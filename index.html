Killer Bunny

Don't be fooled by this little guy's cute exterior, this creature is incredibly dangerous.The killer bunny is carnivorous, and will not hesitate to attack and eat anything, including humans.

They are often found in grassland, though occsasionally you'll find them in colder climates. Make sure to avoid their burrows or any caves they may be hiding in.

Killer bunnies are most active just after sunrise, so be weary of them during those hours. Never wake one up; they are especially vicious when their sleep has been disturbed.
